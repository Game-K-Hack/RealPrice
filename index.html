RealPrice is a practical currency conversion application for travelers. Easily compare exchange rates, adjust bank fees, and calculate costs accurately. Stay updated with automatic internet-connected updates for precise conversions on-the-go.

Description

RealPrice is a currency conversion application essential for travelers who want to know the real cost of items in a foreign currency. With RealPrice, you can easily compare exchange rates between two currencies and adjust the percentage of commission charged by your bank during international transactions. This feature allows you to calculate the final price of an item or service, taking into account not only the exchange rate but also the associated bank fees. The application automatically updates itself when you are connected to the Internet, ensuring accurate and up-to-date conversions. Don't travel without RealPrice anymore! Be sure to know exactly how much you will spend in your chosen currency, taking into account your bank's conversion fees.

Features

Fast Processing

Live calculation of every change in the amount indicated in any entry

Wifi Compatibility

For greater accuracy of the current conversion rate, the application automatically retrieves information from the Internet via Wi-Fi or mobile data

Data accuracy

What makes us different from other apps is our ability to calculate the exchange rate using your bank's commission rate.
